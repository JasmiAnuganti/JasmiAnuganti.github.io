Panic!At the Disco

Panic! At the Disco is an alternative pop-rock band that was founded in 2004 by Ryan Ross, Spencer Smith, Brent Williams and Brendon Urie. Their debut album, called A Fever You Can't Sweat Out, was released in 2005, in which the song I Write Sins Not Tragedies was the most popular among crowds. Over the years, the band has risen to immense fame gaining a large fan base, who call themselves "Sinners". The band recently released their 6th studio album, Pray for the Wicked.
